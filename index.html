Flathead Lake, MT

Nestled in northwest Montana, Flathead Lake is the largest natural freshwater lake west of the Mississippi River and one of the clearest in the United States. Framed by the distant peaks of Glacier National Park and surrounded by charming small towns, orchards, and forested hills, the lake offers a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure. Visitors can spend their days boating, kayaking, fishing for lake trout, or swimming in its remarkably clean waters, while nearby hiking trails and scenic drives showcase the breathtaking beauty of Montana’s “Big Sky Country.”
